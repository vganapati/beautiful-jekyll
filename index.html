I'm a Postdoctoral Associate at Verily (formerly Google[x]) Life Sciences, working on the robotic surgery team. I received my Ph.D in Electrical Engineering & Computer Science at the University of California, Berkeley, advised by Prof. Eli Yablonovitch. My graduate work centered around the optimization and thermodynamics of high efficiency photovoltaics and was supported by the Department of Energy Office of Science Graduate Fellowship and the UC Berkeley Chancellor's Fellowship. My undergraduate research at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology was advised by Prof. Tonio Buonassisi and focused on imaging microcrystalline defects in multicrystalline silicon with infrared birefringence.

I'm interested in applying optimization algorithms to everything, from photovoltaics to medical imaging.
